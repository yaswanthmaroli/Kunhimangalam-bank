About Us

Kunjimanagala Service Co-operative bank was registered on 05/02/1920 at Payyanur, Kannur. On 27/05/1920 it started working as united currency group. From 12/11/1972 onwards it startes working as Service Co-operative bank which obtained 'Special Grade Status' on 01/04/2013. Initially it was a nominal bank which by 20 years, now turned to an important bank in Kannur by diversification in loan. It has 5 branches including main branch at Kannur. It conducts Neethi Electrical shop, Plumbing Shop & festival markets. Innovative loan projects are carried out in the bank. Governing body take care to prepare all modern banking facilities. Currently bank has facilities for Core banking, Western Union Money Transfer & RTGS/NEFT. We provide all type of loans including housing loan, vehicle loan, gold loan etc... with low interest rates. At the same time we provide high interest for deposits. Safe deposit locker is available in all our branches.
